About Us

Lucky Shrub, based in Tucson, Arizona, is a medium-sized garden design firm specializing in creating stunning garden designs, providing expert maintenance, and offering a variety of indoor and outdoor plants through our small plant nursery.

Founded by the passionate plant lovers, Jason and Maria, Lucky Shrub brings together artistic vision and horticultural expertise to craft beautiful outdoor spaces.
